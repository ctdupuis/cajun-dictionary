Word of the Day

Mais Yeah

MAY yeh

Definition:

Showing affirmation to a question that the person answering belives is an obvious answer

Use Case:

"Hey man, did you watch the Saints play yesterday?" "Mais Yeah, I never miss a game,"

Most Recent Addition

Lagniappe

LANyahp

Definition:

The Cajun-French word for extra or excess

Use Case:

"I find people don't season their crawfish the way I like. I like my crawfish with a little bit of lagniappe."
